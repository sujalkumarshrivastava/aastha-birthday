Happy Birthday!

Brown was never my favorite color—until I saw it resting in your eyes.
Black felt too dark to love—until your cloudy hair taught it how to shine.
I used to turn away from harsh white sunlight—until your silvery glow made it feel soft.
I once lived focused on my work—until your smile quietly became my favorite distraction
If beauty had a habit,
it would be distracting people exactly the way you distract me....

Every moment with you feels magical. Your laughter is my favorite melody, and your presence my sweetest comfort.
May your birthday be filled with joy, love, and endless smiles....
